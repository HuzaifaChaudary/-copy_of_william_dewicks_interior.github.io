Hello! I'm William and welcome to my interior design page. I am a residential interior designer based in Nottingham who is here to help you create your perfect space. With a passion for all things interior design and home decor for many years, I am waiting to help you turn your space into the perfect retreat.

 

The part about INTERIORS

Discover your perfection

The part about INSPIRATION

Inspiration for interior design can come from anywhere. A piece of furniture, a colour, a landscape, a design, a piece of art. Through my thorough consultation, I can take that initial spark of inspiration to a full design.

The part about COLOUR

Colour already selected and don't know what to pair it with or don't know where to begin? With my expertise I can show you options and how they would work for you and your space. The choice of colour is extremely personal and can mean different things to different people.

The part about PLANS

Space planning is an essential part of interior design, you don't want to make the mistake of an expensive furniture purchase for it to be too big or too small. I will help to make sure that it is just the right fit for your space.

The part about FURNITURE

Keeping your current furniture or wanting to buy all new? Either way, I can make your room work for your current furniture or help with the sourcing and purchase of new/reclaimed/bespoke.

The part about ACCESSORIES

Showing personality through your accessories can be the make or break of a space. I can help you to understand how to style and personalise a space without it looking like a jumble sale.
